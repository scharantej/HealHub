Mental Health Awareness

It's Okay to Not Be Okay

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It's okay to not be okay sometimes. If you're struggling with your mental health, there are people who can help.

Get Help

If you're in crisis, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room. There are also many resources available to help you get the support you need.

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • The Trevor Project: 1-866-488-7386

Spread the Word

Talking about mental health is important. Help break the stigma by sharing your story or talking to your friends and family about mental health.

Mental health matters. You are not alone.
